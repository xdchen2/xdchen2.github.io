Flappy Bird Auditory Discrimination Test

Welcome to the Flappy Bird auditory discrimination experiment! Please read the instructions carefully:

  1. In each trial, you will hear three sounds as your bird prepares to fly through pipes.
  2. One of the sounds will be different from the other two (either the first sound or the third sound).
  3. Your task is to identify which sound is different by pressing the correct key:

Keyboard Controls:

Press F if the FIRST sound (Sound 1) is different

Press J if the THIRD sound (Sound 3) is different

🔊 Example Trial Walkthrough:

Step 1: You will see the bird preparing to fly and hear three sounds in sequence: Sound 1 → Sound 2 → Sound 3

Step 2: Listen carefully and identify which one sounds different

Step 3: Press F (for Sound 1) or J (for Sound 3)

Step 4: If correct, your bird will fly through successfully! If incorrect, your bird will crash into the pipe.

  1. Correct answers help your bird fly through the pipes and earn points!
  2. Incorrect answers cause your bird to crash into the pipes.
  3. The test will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.
  4. Please ensure you're in a quiet environment and using headphones for best results.

Remember: F = First sound different | J = Third sound different
