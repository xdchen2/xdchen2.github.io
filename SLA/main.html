Standard Test

The standard test includes assessment of auditory dimensions: formant, pitch, duration, and rise time. The test takes approximately 20-30 minutes.

Quick Test

The quick test is a faster way to assess auditory dimensions, though it may be less accurate. It takes approximately 10 to 15 minutes to complete.
